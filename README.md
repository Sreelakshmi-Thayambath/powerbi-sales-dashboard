# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive sales analysis to monitor revenue performance, product profitability, regional sales trends, and business growth opportunities.

## Business Problem

Organizations need real-time visibility into sales performance to:

- Track revenue growth
- Identify top-performing products
- Evaluate regional performance
- Improve profitability
- Support strategic decision-making

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features

### Revenue Analysis
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Year-over-Year Growth

### Product Analysis
- Top Selling Products
- Product Category Performance
- Profit Contribution

### Regional Analysis
- Revenue by Region
- Profit by Region
- Customer Distribution

### Customer Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Repeat Customers
- Customer Contribution

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|-------|------------|
| Total Revenue | Overall sales revenue |
| Total Profit | Net profit generated |
| Profit Margin | Profitability percentage |
| Orders Count | Total transactions |
| Average Order Value | Revenue per order |

## Key Findings

### Finding 1
A small group of products contributes the majority of overall revenue.

### Finding 2
Certain regions consistently outperform others in both revenue and profitability.

### Finding 3
Seasonal trends indicate peak sales during specific months.

### Finding 4
A subset of customers generates a significant portion of revenue.

### Finding 5
Several products have high sales volume but low profit margins.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products.
- Improve inventory planning during peak sales periods.
- Increase investment in high-profit regions.
- Develop retention programs for high-value customers.
- Review pricing strategies for low-margin products.
